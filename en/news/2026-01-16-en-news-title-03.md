Professor Dr. Vivian Vasconcelos Costa, leader of GPAV-UFMG, won the researcher award presented by the Brazil Conference.

The Brazil Conference is the largest conference discussing Brazil outside of the country, bringing together students, researchers, leaders, and professionals to debate Brazil's major challenges and opportunities. In 2026, the conference will reach its 12th edition, taking place on the Harvard and MIT campuses, with the theme: "The Future of Brazil: Transforming Challenges into Progress." A space for dialogue, ideas, and collective construction for a more just, innovative, and inclusive Brazil.
