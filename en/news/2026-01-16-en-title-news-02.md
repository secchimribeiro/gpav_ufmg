In its 12th edition, the UFMG Microbiology Symposium "Jacques Nicoli" presents the theme "Translational Microbiology". The GPAV-UFMG team once again confirmed its significant presence with innovative posters and a lecture by the group's leader, Prof. Dr. Vivian Vasconcelos Costa, on the topic "Long COVID-19 and the immune response".
