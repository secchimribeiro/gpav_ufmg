# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected on the website of the **Research Group on Arboviral Diseases**, affiliated with the Department of Morphology, Institute of Biological Sciences, Federal University of Minas Gerais (UFMG).

## 1. General principles

This website is **institutional, academic, and informational** in nature. We are committed to respecting visitorsâ€™ privacy and handling any personal data in a responsible, transparent, and secure manner, in accordance with applicable data protection principles, including those of the **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)** and similar legislation.

## 2. Collection of personal data

Personal data may be collected **only when voluntarily provided by the user**, specifically through the contact form available on this website. The collected data may include:

- First name
- Last name
- Institution
- Email address
- Message content

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data.

## 3. Purpose of data use

The personal data provided are used exclusively for:

- Responding to inquiries submitted through the contact form
- Maintaining institutional communication related to the activities of the research group

The data are **not used for commercial purposes**, marketing, or unsolicited communications.

## 4. Data sharing

Personal data collected through this website **are not shared with third parties**, except where required by law or regulatory obligations.

## 5. Data storage and security

Messages submitted through the contact form are forwarded to an institutional or designated email address managed by the website administrator. Reasonable technical and organizational measures are taken to protect the information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

## 6. Cookies and tracking technologies

This website **does not use proprietary cookies** for personal identification.

Traffic analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) may be used for statistical purposes and to improve the website. Such tools collect data in an aggregated and, where possible, anonymized manner.

## 7. External links

This website may contain links to external websites (e.g., scientific journals, databases, or social media platforms). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites.

## 8. Data subject rights

You have the right to:

- Request access to your personal data
- Request correction or deletion of your data
- Withdraw consent for data processing

Requests may be submitted via the contact form or through the email address provided on this website.

## 9. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Users are encouraged to review this page periodically to stay informed about any changes.

## 10. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or the handling of personal data, please contact us using the form available on this website.
